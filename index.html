Authentic Experience

THE SOUL OF THE PROVINCE

Binirayan is more than a parade; it is a sacred journey back to the Malandog landing. It honors the arrival of the ten Bornean Datus, whose courage and leadership laid the foundation for the civilization of Antique and the entire island of Panay.

The Malandog Landing

Commemorating the site where the Bornean Datus first anchored their sails.

Ancestral Valor

Preserving the legacy of freedom and culture brought by our founding ancestors.

Where Beauty Meets Tradition

Celebrate the Legacy

This website serves as an informational and promotional platform for the Binirayan Festival, presenting its history, festival activities, schedule of events, cultural highlights, and current updates in a clear and engaging format.

Festival Atmosphere

Where Heritage Comes Alive in Color and Celebration

Binirayan is not only remembered through story - it is felt through music, movement, torch-lighting, street performances, and the shared joy of the Antiqueño community.

From ceremonial reenactments to vibrant public gatherings, the festival transforms heritage into a living experience that welcomes both locals and visitors into the spirit of Antique.

Festival Schedule Preview

Highlights of the Celebration

Experience a vibrant lineup of ceremonial, cultural, and community-centered activities that bring the Binirayan Festival to life.

Day 1

Opening Ceremony

A formal opening program that welcomes guests, communities, and performers to the celebration.

Day 2

Street Dancing & Parade

A colorful showcase of movement, costumes, rhythm, and creative presentations inspired by local culture.

Day 3

Cultural Performances

Stage-based performances featuring storytelling, dance, music, and community artistry.

Day 4

Festival Competitions

Creative contests and public showcases that encourage participation from schools and local groups.

Day 5

Community Activities

Public gatherings, exhibits, and community-centered programs that celebrate Antiqueño identity.

Finale

Closing Program

A concluding celebration of gratitude, pride, reflection, and the continuing spirit of Binirayan.
