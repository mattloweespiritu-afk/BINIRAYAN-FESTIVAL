Visitor Information

Where to go and where to ask

The Contact page should help visitors understand the main festival area, key venues, and where to look for schedule changes or updates.

Main Festival Area

San Jose de Buenavista

The primary public activity center for major festival programs, community exhibits, and visitor-heavy events.

Key Venue

Evelio B. Javier Freedom Park

One of the main public spaces for showcases, public activity, and festival-centered community gatherings.

Heritage Site

Malandog Beach, Hamtic

A heritage-linked site associated with commemorative and symbolic festival activity.

Inquiry Hours

8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
